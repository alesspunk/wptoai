FAQ

Real questions before you start.

What exactly are you migrating?

We migrate your page structure, content, and reusable sections from your current WordPress URL. Your design and brand stay the same.

Do I need to shut down WordPress?

No. Your current WordPress site keeps running while we prepare the migrated system.

How long does it take?

Small and medium sites are usually ready in a few business days. Bigger sites take longer based on page count and complexity.

What do we get at handoff?

You get clean HTML and CSS files with an image folder, sitemap files, a Figma design system, Notion docs, an AI-ready chatbot, Storybook documentation in a GitHub repo, and Vercel deploy setup.

Who owns the final result?

You do. 100% client ownership of files and documentation.

Is our site data private?

Yes. We treat your URL, files, and project details as private and only use them for your migration.

Do you offer monthly maintenance?

Yes. You can add monthly maintenance with AI engineers and prompts for ongoing changes.
